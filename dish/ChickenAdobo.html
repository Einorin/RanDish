ChickenAdobo

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • SoySauce
  • Vinegar
  • MagicSarap

Instructions

  1. Saute Garlic,Ginger and Onion
  2. Add Chicken and fry both side of chicken till golden brown.
  3. Add 3/4 cup each of water,SoySauce and Vinegar
  4. Cook in medium to high-heat for 13-15 minutes
  5. Add boiled egg (optional)
  6. Serve
