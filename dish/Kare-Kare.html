Kare-kare


Ingredients


  • Pork or Beef
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Eggplant
  • Petchay
  • StringBeans
  • PeanutButter
  • AnnatoSeeds

Instructions

  1. Mise en place(prepare all ingredients). Soak the AnnatoSeeds in around 1 cup of hot water this is for the color of the dish.
  2. Saute Onion for 20 seconds, followed by Garlic in the pot.
  3. Saute the meat to give it color you may add salt and pepper.
  4. Add water enough to submerge the meat
  5. Slow cook for 40 to 60 minutes
  6. After step 5, separate the AnnatoSeeds from water then add the colored water in the pot
  7. Add PeanutButter 3-4 tablespoons is enough for 1 kilo of meat.
  8. After 2-3 minutes Add the vegetables, we don't want to overcooked it so, simmer it for around 3-5 minutes and serve. (We can also add slurry mix if we want the sauce to be thick)
