Pork Adobo

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Sprite
  • SoySauce
  • OysterSauce
  • Vinegar
  • BlackPepper
  • BayLeaves
  • Potato

Instructions

  1. In a pan, Saute Onion and Garlic
  2. Add the pork and saute it for about 4-5 minutes
  3. Add the BayLeaves and BlackPepper
  4. Add 3 tbsp of SoySauce and OysterSauce
  5. Add sprite enough to submerge the meat
  6. Slow cook in medium to low heat for 45 minutes, add water if needed.
  7. Add the potato
  8. Now this is the fun part! Separate the meat from the sauce and pan fry the meat
  9. After pan frying combine the sauce and meat together in the pan and add 2 tbsp vinegar and simmer for 4 minutes
